Verification

Why our questions stay right

The most common complaint about trivia apps isn't difficulty. It's the wrong answer. We built Quiz Tempo around that problem.

Reviewed, not improvised

Our questions run through a verification engine: answers cross-checked, sources controlled, duplicates removed. Theme by theme, continuously.

Corrected, never deleted

A wrong question gets fixed, not thrown away. The catalogue gains quality without ever losing volume.

Kept current

A transfer, a broken record, an election: yesterday's right answer is today's wrong one. We go back over it.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Quiz Tempo free?

Yes. The game is free and ad-supported. The optional premium subscription removes ads and unlocks extra bonuses.

Which platforms can I play on?

iPhone and iPad from iOS 17, and Android from version 8. The game is available in English, French, and Spanish.

Can I play alone or with friends?

Both. Solo play, the daily challenge, and the leaderboards need no one else. You can also add friends and send them a challenge on the theme of your choice.

Where do the questions come from?

They are written, then reviewed by a verification engine that cross-checks answers, controls sources, and catches questions that have gone out of date. A wrong question gets corrected, never deleted.

Does the game refresh regularly?

The daily challenge changes theme every day, and the daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly leaderboards renew continuously.
