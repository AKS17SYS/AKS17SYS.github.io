Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 16, 2025

Overview

Quiz Tempo (“we”) provides a quiz game on iOS, Android, and web. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • App analytics (usage events, device info) to improve features and stability.
  • Advertising data if you use the free version (e.g., via AdMob/partners).
  • Account & gameplay (e.g., nickname, scores, achievements) to enable multiplayer and leaderboards.

How We Use Data

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the app and services.
  • Provide matchmaking, leaderboards, rewards, and anti-abuse protections.
  • Show ads (if applicable) and measure their performance.

Third-Party Services

We may use services such as Firebase (Analytics, Firestore, Messaging) and AdMob for ads. These providers process data under their own terms and policies.

Your Choices

  • Opt-out of analytics/ads personalization where supported by your device settings.
  • Request deletion of your account data by contacting us at help.aksyesytem@gmail.com.

Children

The game is intended for a general audience. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us to remove it.
