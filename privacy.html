Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 4, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Quiz Tempo mobile application (the “Application”), developed by Akmal Ahmed (the “Service Provider”). The Application is provided as an ad-supported service on an “AS IS” basis.

Information We Collect

The Application may collect information you provide directly or through your use of the service, including:

  • your player name or display name;
  • your country selection or approximate location-derived country for rankings and regional features;
  • your profile photo, if you choose to upload one;
  • your in-app messages and friend or social interactions;
  • your gameplay data, such as game history, scores, rankings, streaks, badges, inventory, and progression;
  • purchase-related status, such as premium entitlement or purchase restoration state;
  • technical information needed to operate the Application, such as device identifiers, push notification tokens, app version, and basic delivery or service information from our providers.

Some features rely on an account or player identity layer, including Game Center, Firebase Authentication, and your in-app profile. If you do not use those features, some parts of the Application may be unavailable.

Location Information

The Application does not collect precise real-time GPS location.

The Application may use approximate location information, or information you provide about your country, to:

  • place you in country or regional rankings;
  • display localized or region-relevant content and competitions;
  • support fraud prevention, safety, and service integrity.

How We Use Information

  • create and maintain your player profile;
  • provide gameplay features, multiplayer, leaderboards, streaks, rewards, and social features;
  • store and display your selected profile photo, avatars, frames, badges, and preferences;
  • deliver push notifications and important service-related updates;
  • process purchases, premium access, and restore transactions;
  • show ads, manage consent choices, and operate ad-supported features;
  • secure the Application, prevent abuse, troubleshoot issues, and improve the service.

Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that may collect or process information in accordance with their own privacy policies, including:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Firebase
  • Apple Game Center

These services may support authentication, cloud data storage, messaging, notifications, content delivery, purchases, and advertising.

Advertising and Consent

The Application may display advertisements through third-party advertising partners such as AdMob. Depending on your region and choices, you may be asked for consent regarding personalized or non-personalized advertising. Advertising partners may process device-level data as described in their own privacy documentation.

Sharing of Information

We may share information:

  • with service providers that help us operate the Application;
  • when required by law or valid legal process;
  • when reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, users, or the integrity of the service;
  • in connection with fraud prevention, abuse prevention, or security investigations.

Some service providers may process user-linked data where necessary to provide the service.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to operate the Application, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain core account or gameplay functionality. Some information may remain in backups or logs for a limited period. If you want your data deleted, contact help.aks.system@gmail.com. Certain data may need to be retained where required for security, legal, transactional, or operational reasons.

Your Choices

  • uninstall the Application to stop future collection from the device;
  • manage notification permissions through your device settings;
  • manage tracking or advertising consent where supported;
  • contact the Service Provider to request deletion or ask privacy questions.

The Application is not intended for children under 13, and the Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, contact help.aks.system@gmail.com so appropriate action can be taken.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of October 4, 2025.
